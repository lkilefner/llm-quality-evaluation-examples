# Judge Rubric — K–12 Explanations & Feedback (1–5)

**1. Accuracy**
1 = major factual errors; 3 = mostly correct; 5 = fully correct & precise

**2. Alignment to Instructions & Audience**
1 = ignores instructions/grade level; 3 = partially aligned; 5 = follows all constraints, grade-appropriate

**3. Tone & Safety**
1 = negative/unsafe language; 3 = neutral, acceptable; 5 = supportive, inclusive, growth-oriented

**4. Clarity & Cognitive Load**
1 = confusing, dense; 3 = adequate clarity; 5 = clear, well-structured, simple language

**5. Actionability (when applicable)**
1 = vague, not useful; 3 = some guidance; 5 = concrete next steps or clear summary

**Scoring Guidance**
- Overall Score: average of available criteria (round to nearest whole)
- Use comments to note failure modes (missing constraints, jargon, off-level, etc.)
